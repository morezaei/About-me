Research Interests
Radiation Testing
Analyzing the effects of radiation on electronic components and systems, particularly related to Single Event Upsets (SEUs) and their impact on reliability.
SRAM Analysis
Studying the behavior of Static Random Access Memory (SRAM) under radiation exposure and developing techniques to improve fault tolerance.
Fault Tolerance
Researching and implementing strategies to enhance the resilience of computer systems against hardware failures, with a focus on software and architectural solutions.
Research Projects
READAR-II
Developing hardware and software techniques to analyze, detect, and recover from radiation-induced errors in embedded digital systems for space missions.
RESHYLIENCE
Studying the effects of radiation and efficient processing of hyperspectral images for "new space" applications, like small satellite technology.
Funding and Collaboration
Both projects are funded by the Ministry of Economy and Competitiveness and conducted at the Complutense University of Madrid.
